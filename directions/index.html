Directions

The D-LOCKS 2024 workshop will be hosted by DTU/DAWN at:

Anker Engelunds Vej 101, 2800 Kongens Lyngby

Meeting room M1, which can be found on the 1st floor.

There are several options for reaching DTU from Copenhagen airport and central station.

  • CPH airport to Nørreport: Metro-line M2 connects the airport to Nørreport station in the city centre. From Nørreport bus 150S and 15E can take you to DTU and stops ca. 300m from building 101 (Rævehøjvej DTU (Helsingørmotorvejen))
  • CPH airport to Copenhagen Central (Københavns hovedbanegård): Øresundstog runs between Copenhagen airport and Copenhagen Central Station. From Copenhagen Central (and Nørreport) runs the S-tran lines A and E to Lyngby. From Lyngby Station, DTU can be reached either by foot (20-30min walk), or by bus 300S, 181 or 190.

Use rejseplanen.dk for exact departure times, a map and connections.
