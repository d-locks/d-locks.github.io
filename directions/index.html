Directions

The D-LOCKS 2024 workshop will be hosted by DTU/DAWN in building 101A meeting room M1, on the first floor across the hallway from the administration. A map to the venue can be seen below.

Several methods for travelling between Copenhagen and DTU exist. From the airport, the metro-line M2 connects to Nørreport where both Bus 150S and 15E can take you 300m from building 101 (Rævehøjvej DTU (Helsingørmotorvejen)). Otherwise, the S-train line A connects to Lyngby Station from where bus 300S takes you to DTU (Rævehøjvej DTU (Lundtoftegårdsvej)). Use rejseplanen.dk for exact departure times, a map and connections.
